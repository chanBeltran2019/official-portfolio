I am Chantal Beltran,

I am a senior at the University of Central Florida and will earn my Digital Media Bachelor's Degree on the Web Design track in the Spring of 2022. Not knowing what I wanted to do when I came out of high-school, I somehow found myself where I belong, in the world of coding and designing. Coming from a family of computer engineers and designers, it only came naturally to me to choose this route. My passion for the industry stems beyond just coding and designing. The idea that I can help ease everyday life for people and make a difference in the content that I produce excites and motivates me beyond reason.

I consider myself a very involved student and always looking for opportunities to grow and learn. Yearning for growth, I joined the Nicholson School of Communication and Media Alumni Mentorship Program. Through this mentor and mentee relationship, I get to learn from a Project Manager who oversees Front-End and Back-End Developers, as well as Designers. This experience has been greatly beneficial, as I am more confident going into the workforce knowing more about how the industry works.

The workforce is not just about technical experience, however. Part of being a good employee and co-worker is the ability to communicate and work as part of a team. During my senior year of college, I participated in multiple clubs. One of these clubs, SHPE at UCF, taught me how to work with others to achieve a shared goal and how to express ideas in a clear manner. Having the skill to communicate well has been undervalued and it is a skill that many do not have. Knowing this, I took the initiative to put myself in situations where communication was essential and have learned to do it efficiently.
